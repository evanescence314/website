VMT Lettering

For the 2017-2018 season, lettering students must have at least 20 service hours, as well as attend 2/3 of the VMT 8th period meetings. This may come from TJIMO, Mathcounts, or lectures. However, note that you cannot say you spent 20 hours writing a lecture and count all 20 as service hours!! Each lecture can count as a maximum of 2 hours: 1 hour of preparation and 1 hour of actual lecturing.

If there is anything wrong with the rankings, please email vmtofficers@gmail.com to let us know to fix the issue.
